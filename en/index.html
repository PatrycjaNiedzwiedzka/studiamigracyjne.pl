Jagiellonian Center for Studies on Migration

UJ

Who are we?

Great importance of migration processes and their multidimensionality was the reason for funding the Jagiellonian Center for Studies on Migration. JCSM provides a coordination platform for the migration researchers working in different units of the university. It also helps to establish a closer cooperation with the employees of other universities in the academic community of Cracow and southeastern Poland who deal with spatial mobility of people. The Centre will help to build an interdisciplinary team that would be able to examine the processes of migration in their multifaceted complexity.

What we do?

The aim of the Centre is to initiate, organize and coordinate the various forms of scientific activity in the field of interdisciplinary research on migration processes as well as activities related to education on the phenomena and processes related to migration.

Founding the JCSM

Resolution No. 133 / VI / 2013 of Jagiellonian University's Senate on June 26, 2013 on: the creation of the Jagiellonian Centre for Studies on Migration and the adoption of the Statute of the Jagiellonian Centre for Studies on Migration.
